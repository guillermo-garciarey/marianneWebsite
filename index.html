About Me

Hi there

I am Marianne, a qualified personal trainer. I finished my studies in 2020, becoming an ITEC-qualified personal trainer.

I worked for a number of years at Hustle Fitness Gym in Ennis, Co. Clare. Over the past 4–5 years, I have gained experience working with clients who have different goals, such as bikini competitions, sports performance, dance training, or simply weight loss and toning. I have also worked with clients recovering from injuries or major health conditions.

Even though I have trained both women and men, I specialize in women's health, tailored plyometric training for specific sports, and functional movement patterns.

Certification Number - 603/4483/0

My Services

MBM Fitness is a space I created as a coach, considering the different needs, goals, and health backgrounds of my previous clients.

This is personal training — not a pre-designed program, but one that adjusts to you holistically, considering your availability, physical condition, and personality.

As your coach, I am committed to bringing you into the world of fitness with accountability and a great experience that fits into your lifestyle while helping you reach your goals.

If you're intermediate or advanced but feel lost on where to start again, I'm here to help you too.

What You Get:

  • Free consultation
  • No extra gym fees
  • Biometric follow-up, measurements (if required), and nutrition support
  • 1-hour training sessions
  • Different training styles to meet your needs

Areas of Focus:

  • Body realignment
  • Working around injuries
  • Injury recovery
  • Women's health
  • Weight loss
  • Fine motor recovery
  • Special needs and elderly fitness (adults and kids, facilities to be agreed)
